Analysis of Covid-19 Evolution Across Mexico City

4th June 2021

The present figure shows the evolution of average active daily Covid-19 cases across all Mexico City throughout 2020. As we can see, the city presented a dramatic increase in cases towards the Q4 of 2020. There is a particular peak in early December 2020 due to a correction in the statistical data input from SINAVE, the governmental office in charge of epidemiological watch for the city. The increase in cases matches the relaxation of lockdown measures and the imposed policy of "back to normal".
