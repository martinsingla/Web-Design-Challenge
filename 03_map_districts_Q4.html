Analysis of Covid-19 Evolution Across Mexico City

4th June 2021

By the end of 2020 (Q4) it was clear that Covid-19 cases had peaked all over the city. However, it was also most notably clear that the most severely afected districts where those more socially vulnerable, with a higher level of low-income household density and informality in the economic activity.
