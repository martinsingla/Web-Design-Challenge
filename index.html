Analysis of Covid-19 Evolution Across Mexico City

4th June 2021

The present project explores some of the most relevant metrics on Covid-19 evolution across Mexico City in 2020. We have examined the reported average active daily cases across the entire city as well as by district and borough. Daily reports are published by the Ministry of Health of the Municipal Government, and is describes estimated active cases. The disease seems to have afected with more severity some of the most vulnerable areas of the city. District like Iztapalapa or Álvaro Obregón have been the epicenter of the pandemic in Mexico City in late 2020.
